This API contains two endpoints that consume machine learning models to analyze text related to products. Here's how to use them:

1. Sentiment Prediction

Predicts the overall sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) of Moroccan people about a product.

  • Endpoint: /predict_sentiment
  • Method: POST
  • JSON Parameters:
    • "product_name": The name of the product.
    • "brand_name": The brand of the product.

2. Review Analysis

Analyzes a detailed review of a product based on a model trained on Moroccan product reviews.

  • Endpoint: /analyze_review
  • Method: POST
  • JSON Parameters:
    • "product_name": The name of the product.
    • "brand_name": The brand of the product.
    • "price": The price of the product.
    • "date": The date of the review.
    • "rating": The rating given (e.g., 1-5).
    • "language": The language of the review ("Arabic" or "Darija").
    • "review_text": The actual text of the review.
